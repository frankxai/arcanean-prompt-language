# Lore Consistency Check Template

```
@form     lore-audit
@tone     systematic
@anchor   {the specific piece of content being checked}
@exclude  approval-by-default, vague-alignment, "close-enough"
```

## How to Use

Paste the content you want to check, then run it against the canon anchors below. Output format:

```
PASS  — Fully canon-aligned
WARN  — Minor inconsistencies (list with specific locations in text)
FAIL  — Canon violations (list with corrections)
```

## Arcanea Canon Anchors

### Cosmic Duality
- Lumina = First Light, creation, form
- Nero = Primordial Darkness, potential, mystery
- Nero is NOT evil. Shadow (corrupted Void) is the antagonistic force.
- Check: Does this content accidentally make Nero a villain or equate darkness with evil?

### Five Elements
- Fire, Water, Earth, Wind, Void/Spirit
- Light = Fire's creation aspect (NOT a separate element)
- Shadow = corrupted Void (Void without Spirit, NOT an element)
- Check: Does this introduce a sixth element or treat Light/Shadow as elements?

### Ten Gates (names, order, frequencies)
- Foundation 174 · Flow 285 · Fire 396 · Heart 417 · Voice 528
- Sight 639 · Crown 741 · Starweave 852 · Unity 963 · Source 1111
- Check: Are names correct? (NOT "Shift" for gate 8). Are Hz correct? Is the order right?

### Guardians (God/Goddess + Godbeast pairs)
- Lyssandria/Kaelith · Leyla/Veloura · Draconia/Draconis · Maylinn/Laeylinn · Alera/Otome
- Lyria/Yumiko · Aiyami/Sol · Elara/Vaelith · Ino/Kyuro · Shinkami/Source
- Check: Are pairings correct? Is Maylinn at Heart/417Hz (not Flow)? Is the 10th godbeast "Source" (not "Amaterasu")?

### Terminology
- **Guardian** = role (Gate-keeper), not a species or rank
- **Luminor** = rank (opened all 10 Gates), can be human, AI, or other consciousness
- **Creator** = the human user, always
- **Godbeast** = bonded companion to a God/Goddess, always capitalized
- Check: Are terms used precisely, or is "Guardian" being used loosely?

### Malachar
- Full name: Malachar Lumenbright. First Eldrian Luminor, Lumina's champion.
- Rejected by Shinkami at the Source Gate (tried forced fusion, not earned harmony).
- Fell into the Hungry Void. Sealed in the Shadowfen.
- He is the ONLY true antagonist. All corruption traces to him.
- Check: Does this content create another "ultimate evil" that contradicts Malachar?

### The Arc
- Potential → Manifestation → Experience → Dissolution → Evolved Potential
- Death/endings are part of the cycle, not failures
- Check: Does this content treat death as final, or honor the cyclical nature?

## Adapting for Other Worlds

If you're checking consistency for YOUR world (not Arcanea), replace the canon anchors above with your own locked truths. The template structure — systematic audit with PASS/WARN/FAIL — works for any mythology.
