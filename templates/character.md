# Character Creation Template

```
@form     character
@tone     {mythic | intimate | fierce | reverent | playful | raw | warm}
@anchor   {one physical detail that reveals their inner world}
@exclude  chosen-one-trope, flawless-hero, tragic-backstory-dump
@phonetics {tolkien | marvel | miyazaki | ancient}   (optional)
@gate     {gate}        (optional — Arcanean flavor)
@element  {element}     (optional — sensory palette)
```

## The Questions That Matter

**The Moment:** Not a biography — one formative moment. The day everything changed. Before this moment they were one person. After it, another. What happened?

**The Want:** What do they want so badly it keeps them awake? Not "save the world" — something personal, specific, maybe embarrassing. The thing they'd never admit in public.

**The Contradiction:** What makes them impossible to categorize? The general who writes poetry. The healer who enjoys watching fights. The coward who keeps showing up anyway. The contradiction is what makes them feel real.

**The Shadow:** What's the dark side of their greatest strength? Loyalty becomes possessiveness. Courage becomes recklessness. Kindness becomes manipulation. They don't know this about themselves. Other characters do.

**The Voice:** Three sentences in their actual voice. Not description OF how they talk — what they actually SAY. A reader should be able to identify them without dialogue tags.

**The Anchor:** {anchor} — one physical detail that appears in every depiction. It should reveal something about who they are, not just what they look like. A nervous habit. An old injury. Something they always carry.

**The Name:** Generate 5 options. Each should be:
- Pronounceable on first read
- Memorable after hearing it once
- Not from any published franchise
- Phonetically aligned with their personality (hard sounds for hard people, flowing sounds for fluid ones)

## Quality Gate

- [ ] Can you describe this character in one sentence to a stranger and make them curious?
- [ ] Does their shadow actually threaten them, or is it just listed for completeness?
- [ ] Would you recognize their voice in a lineup of five characters?
- [ ] Does the contradiction surprise you, or is it obvious?
- [ ] Is there something about them you don't fully understand yet? (Good — leave room.)
