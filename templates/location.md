# Location Template

```
@form     location
@tone     {reverent | eerie | warm | ancient | living | decaying}
@anchor   {one sensory detail a visitor notices in the first three seconds}
@exclude  generic-fantasy, empty-spectacle, infinite-grandeur
@element  {element}     (optional — sensory palette)
```

## The Questions That Matter

**Arrival:** What hits you in the first three seconds? Not the history — the raw sensory experience. What does the air taste like? What sound is constant? Is the ground soft or hard under your feet? What temperature is it on your skin?

**Function:** Every real place DOES something. A forge makes weapons. A market exchanges value. A bedroom is where you're most vulnerable. What happens here? What is this place FOR, not what does it look like?

**Tension:** What is wrong here, or contested, or fragile? A place without tension is a postcard. Maybe the foundation is cracking. Maybe two factions claim it. Maybe it's slowly being consumed by something beautiful.

**History in the Walls:** One detail that proves this place is older than the current story. A worn step. A name scratched into stone. A tree growing through a roof that was once a ceiling. History should be FOUND, not explained.

**Time of Day:** This place should feel different at dawn than at midnight. Describe it at the time that reveals its truest nature.

**Scale:** Intimate (a room, a clearing) or vast (a city, a continent)? The AI needs to know whether to think in footsteps or horizons.

**Anchor:** {anchor} — the first thing any visual or narrative depiction must include. Engage at least two senses.

## Quality Gate

- [ ] Could you draw a rough map from this description?
- [ ] Does it feel lived-in, or like a movie set waiting for actors?
- [ ] Is there a reason to come here AND a reason to leave?
- [ ] If you removed the name, could someone guess the mood from the description alone?
- [ ] Would it change the story if you set the same scene somewhere else? (If not, it's not specific enough.)
